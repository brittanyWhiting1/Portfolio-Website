Jolt Coffee

Our team completed this capstone as the final requirement for our coding Bootcamp, Tech Elevator. Jolt is a coffee shop finder which we created using C# , ASP .NET, SQL Server, Vue.js, and CSS. While working on this site, we utilized the principles of Agile methodology, which enabled us to deliver the project on time and with high-quality results. Each of our four sprints were completed in 2-3 days. We are proud of the outcome of Jolt, as well as the teamwork and dedication that went into the process of creating it.
